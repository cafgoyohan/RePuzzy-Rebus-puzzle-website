About RePuzzy

RePuzzy is a Filipino Rebus Puzzle Platform aimed at enhancing literary comprehension among learners. The platform provides an interactive and enjoyable learning experience through game-based educational content.

Objectives

  • To create an interactive and accessible platform for an enjoyable literacy learning experience
  • To help improve Filipino literacy comprehension among students
  • To provide students with a break from school work through simple, quick entertainment
  • To test players' knowledge of Filipino words, expressions, and phrases
  • To improve users' cognitive skills, including memory retention, problem-solving, and logical thinking

Why RePuzzy?

RePuzzy combines the fun of rebus puzzles with educational value, making it an ideal tool for both learning and entertainment. It helps strengthen memory, foster creative problem-solving skills, and enhance logical thinking abilities.
